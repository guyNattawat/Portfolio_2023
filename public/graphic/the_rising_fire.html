the pandemic

Digital art painting work (Personal Project)

The Rising Fire is a personal project inspired by the war in Ukraine in 2022. This artwork aims to represent the brutality of war, likening it to a destructive fire that engulfs a beautiful field of flowers.
The primary objective of this project is to challenge my abilities and provide an opportunity to practice and review techniques and methodologies related to creating concept art, storytelling, digital painting, lighting, and basics of Unreal Engine.


** All of the objects in this project were downloaded from Quixel Megascans **

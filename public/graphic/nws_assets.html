Senior Project Assets

Naval Warfare Online Simulation assets are a crucial component of a senior project. These assets consist of ship models designed specifically for games.
The models are replicas of real ships stationed in the Royal Thai Navy, including FS-441 HTMS Rattanakosin, FFG-455 HTMS Chao Phraya, FFG-457 HTMS Kraburi and FFG-471 HTMS Bhumibol Adulyadej. It accurately depict both the shape of the ships and the equipment installed on board.

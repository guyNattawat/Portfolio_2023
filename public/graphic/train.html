alstom locomotive and third class train

Personal Project

The Alstom locomotive and Third class train project was undertaken to challenge myself in creating a realistic train model including Alstom Locomotive and Third Class passenger car specifically designed for Unreal Engine 5.
The project aimed to provide an opportunity to practice realistic modeling and texturing techniques for game engines, as well as create materials tailored for Unreal Engine 5.

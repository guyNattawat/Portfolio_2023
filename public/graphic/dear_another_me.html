dear another me

Advanced Game Development Project (3 members)

Dear Another Me" is an animated short film that is part of the Computer and Animation subject. Inspired in part by the Pixar animation titled "Inside Out". This short film will tell the story through the main character, who is a girl that will experience a miracle that happens to her. And this project also presents in terms of the beauty of the scenes, characters, and scenes details.
This project is a project that builds experience and skills using Autodesk Maya and techniques such as modeling, rigging, UV mapping, texture, animation pipeline, and render pipeline. And also enhancing the experience of working with others with a focus on the animation pipeline.


Responsible:

DIRECTOR MODELER & TEXTURE RIGGING & BLENDSHAPE
LIGHTING ARTIST ANIMATOR COMPOSITOR & EDITOR

the pandemic

Digital art painting work (Personal Project)

The Pandemic project is a personal project inspired by the outbreak of COVID-19. This piece of art aims to represent the fear experienced by people during the pandemic. The primary objective of this project is to challenge my abilities and provide an opportunity to practice and review techniques and methodologies related to modeling, texturing, lighting, and rendering using Autodesk Maya.


** A main characters in this project were downloaded from 3D marketplace **

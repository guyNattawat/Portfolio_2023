the pandemic

Digital art painting work (Personal Project)

The Quiet Place is a personal project inspired by the COVID-19 pandemic in Thailand. This artwork aims to depict the control of news, where the information presented is disconnected from the reality unfolding at that moment, and the truth remains unspoken.
The primary objective of this project is to challenge my abilities and provide an opportunity to practice and review techniques and methodologies related to creating concept art, storytelling, digital painting, and lighting.


** All of the objects in this project were downloaded from Quixel Megascans **

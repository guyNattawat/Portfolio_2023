naval warfare online simulation

Senior Project (2 members)

Naval Warfare Online Simulation is a multiplayer naval strategy simulation game developed by Unreal engine 4. The game combines the thrilling experience of naval battles with realistic weapon system operations and AI-controlled elements. The game offers various features, including multiplayer functionality, 3D and 2D rendering, real-time strategy game controls, and the ability to freely control and choose from 17 ships and 22 different sets of weapons. Each weapon operates based on its unique principles.
The game also includes gameplay systems such as unit deployment, unit editing, group formation, unit and weapon control, and in-game time control, which enhance the player's ability to comfortably simulate battles.
Significantly, the game has received cooperation from The Royal Navy Thailand, with the shared objective of creating a training simulator tailored specifically for navy personnel. This collaboration further enhances the authenticity and practicality of the game's naval warfare experience.

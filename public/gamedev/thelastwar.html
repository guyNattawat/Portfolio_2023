the last war

Personal Project

"The Last War" is a personal project in which I aim to create a fascinating environment scene and cinematic inspired by "The Division 2" game. The purpose of this project is to challenge myself to learn about 3D Magascan and exterior lighting techniques. The goal is to create a visually stunning environment and cinematic that captures the essence of the game's atmosphere. Overall, the project serves as a means to develop and showcase my skills and knowledge in 3D environment design and cinematography.
This project has a short story: In 2034, the world is facing a new virus that is spread by money bills during Black Friday. It's a devastating pandemic that has killed many people around the world. This scene is made to represent the last human base in London.

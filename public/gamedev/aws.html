Freelance Project

Air Combat Warfare Online Simulation is an expansion game of the Naval Warfare Online Simulation. It combines the thrilling experience of air combat battles and emphasizes the functionality of AIwithin the game.
Operating as a multiplayer game, it introduces new features tailored for Air Force personnel, including air base control, flight schedule control, mission target control, and the ability to adjust capabilities within the game. The game aims to provide a realistic and immersive experience for players engaged in air combat scenarios.
